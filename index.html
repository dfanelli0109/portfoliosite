ABOUT DANIEL

BIO

From even my younger days, I have always had an eye for creativity. My interests have evolved over the years from being behind a camera and video/audio production, journalism, and working with data into web Development. In high school and at the University of Central Florida, I studied digital communications, advertising, business management/accounting and took my first programming class in Pascal. After graduating from college,I operated a special events and wedding videography company, Majestic Moments Digital Studios, where I had the opportunity to maintain my company website and get familiar with some of the front end languages and search engine optimization. Throughout the years, I have always had an eye for creativity and problem solving.

In 2017, I was introduced to an opportunity to learn web development and with a wife newly pregnant, I jumped at the opportunity. At Woz-U, I completed their program and learned a lot and earned my certification as a Full Stack Software Engineer.

My work experience includes sales, producing videography on DVDs with an interactive menu, providing technical support, medical billing/revenue cycle management, data management/analysis, web development and search engine optimization. If you are looking for a website or if your website isn't bringing in traffic that you would like, please contact me for a free consultation. I have received previous certifications in CompTIA A+, CompTIA Network+, Full Stack Software Engineer, and HFMA Certified Revenue Cycle Representative.
